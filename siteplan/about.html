About South Sudan Digital Hub

Our mission to transform South Sudan through technology and innovation

Our Mission

South Sudan Digital Hub is dedicated to connecting young people with the digital skills, knowledge, and opportunities they need to succeed in the 21st century economy. We believe that technology and digital literacy are fundamental tools for breaking cycles of poverty and creating sustainable economic growth in South Sudan.

Through partnerships with tech companies, educational institutions, and government agencies, we provide practical training, job opportunities, and resources that empower our youth to participate in the global digital economy while contributing to local innovation.

Our Vision

A South Sudan where every young person has access to digital skills training, quality employment opportunities, and the tools to build their own tech ventures. We envision a nation where innovation flourishes, entrepreneurs thrive, and technology serves as a bridge to economic prosperity for all.
